How It Works

1. Click "Send Love to Friend" to light up your friend's bracelet

2. Your friend will see their bracelet light up with colorful beads

3. Your friend can click "Send Love to You" to light up your bracelet

4. The connection status shows if you're both online

In a real implementation, this would work across different devices using WebSockets or a similar technology!
